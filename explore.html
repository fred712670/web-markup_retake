Discover the beauty of Haarlem

Het Wed

For the ones wanting to take a bath on a sunny day, the lake “Het Wed” is just right. Surrounded by sand makes this lake really beach alike. The entrance is free and it is the perfect spot to relax in nature.

Oranjekom

For those seeking a little more adventure the lake Oranjekom is just right. It is a very quiet spot surrounded by beautiful vegetation and animals such as deer’s which can be seen at a quite close distance. Around the lake you can take the pedestrian path and experience the beauty of Haarlem.
