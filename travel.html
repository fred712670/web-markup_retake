How to arrive in Haarlem?

Haarlem is conveniently located and easily accessible from various parts of the Netherlands and beyond:

From Amsterdam

Haarlem is just a 15-minute train ride from Amsterdam Central Station

From Schiphol Airport

If you're flying into Amsterdam's Schiphol Airport, you can reach Haarlem within 20 minutes by train or bus.

By Car

You can also reach Haarlem by car, and there are parking facilities available in and around the city. Consider using electric or hybrid vehicles for eco-conscious travel.
